About me

Programmer. Badminton Player. Boy Scout.

Welcome to my personal website! I am Edwin Mui, a current student at Lick-Wilmerding High School. I am passionate about graphic design, coding/programming, and the multi-faceted world of video gaming. In the past, I have taken numerous online courses and hackathons and created my own games. I am proficient in Python, HTML, and Java. I am also the president of the Game Design Club at my school, loves to play badminton, and spends a lot of time outdoors.

Experiences

Where has my interests led me?

  • Paid Intern at Mirada Technologies, inc.

    Starting in summer of 2022 I worked a NSF-sponsered internship under UC Berkley and MIT Doctorates. I researched and developed new production methods for optical scanners, wrote automation scripts, and assembled scanners.

  • Boy Scouts

    As both an Eagle Scout and Senior Patrol Leader, I have participated in numerous activites since 2016. Boy Scouts of America has allowed teens like me to take on leadership positions and help the community. I currently have 34 merit badges, and am Eagle. I am Senior Patrol Leader, in which I ran and organized the troop meetings during the pandemic.

  • Thesecondopinion Technology Volunteer Intern

    Thesecondopinion is a non-profit organization that helps cancer patients seeking a second doctor's opinion on treatment. I was tasked to migrate and organize online database of about 4000 patients, create online questionnaires, maintain their website. Additionally I have also helped fundraise for their annual golf event for cancer.

  • Homework Mentor

    Since 2019, I volunteer regularly at the Burlingame Public Library Homework Center. I tutor kids in a variety of subjects. I additionally taught English to Chinese and Spanish speakers. I helped initiate the migration to online zoom sessions since the start of the pandemic.

  • Youth Leadership and Technology Program

    In the fall of 2021, I was selected out of a competitive pool of 40 applicants to take part in this Comcast-sponsored program. Over the course of the program, I have volunteered 40+ hours in various events, including a vaccine awareness event for seniors by Self-Help for the Elderly. Additionally, I learned about finance management, computer hardware and software, and leadership skills.

  • Badminton

    As the team-captain and MVP of my school team, I have helped organize funding and lead team meetings and worked with coaches to determine logistics. I also serve as assistant coach for the team, and have won awards in the league and regional levels.
